    • DSDM- Dynamic Systems Development Method

    An agile code development approach that provides a framework for building and maintaining systems. DSDM is an iterative code method within which every iteration follows the rule that simply enough work is needed for every increment to facilitate movement to the following increment.

    Eight principles of DSDM

    • Focus on the business need
    • Deliver on time
    • Collaborate
    • Never compromise quality
    • Build incrementally from firm foundations
    • Develop iteratively
    • Communicate continuously and clearly
    • Demonstrate control

    DSDM is designed to be easily tailored and used in conjunction with traditional methods such as PRINCE2 or to complement other Agile approaches such as Scrum.
